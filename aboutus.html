About Majisa Retail Pvt. Ltd

What began as a single supermarket in 1987 is now a hyperlocal supermarket with over 60 stores across Hyderabad and Bengaluru. Majisa Supermarket has become a household name when it comes to quality groceries and a familiar landmark for the cities we operate out of.

How did we manage this?
From farm to fork, our story is one of growth. Not just our own, but the very ecosystem that we are part of.

We maintain a wide assortment of products that include a large range of locally sourced produce. By working with women self-help groups, farmers and government schemes, we ensure that our community grows with us.

We value our customer’s time as much as their money. Our stores are mapped and designed for utmost efficiency to reduce bottlenecks or waiting time at checkout counters. By ensuring that our well-stocked stores maintain the three pillars of Quality, Variety and Savings, Majisa Supermarkets now boasts of a super-loyal clientele who trust us with their eyes closed and shop with their arms open.
